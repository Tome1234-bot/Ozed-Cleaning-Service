What does a weekly home cleaning service include?

Professional, thorough, and methodical regular house cleaning. Safe, eco-friendly products for families and pets. Attention to high-touch areas and hygiene standards. Consistent results on every visit for recurring cleaning.

Kitchen

  • Loading/unloading dishwasher
  • Clean and polish all surfaces
  • Remove spider webs and dust
  • Wipe microwave and kettle in and outside
  • Wipe coffee machine, extractor fan, oven and fridge externally
  • Hoover and mop the floor
  • Empty and re-bag bins

Bathroom and Toilet

  • Clean and polish bathtub/shower, taps, sink, and toilet
  • Polish mirrors
  • Wipe down all surfaces, light switches, doors and skirting boards
  • Hoover and mop the floor
  • Empty and re-bag bins

Living room & common areas

  • Dust, wipe and polish all surfaces, doors, light switches, and skirting boards
  • Hoover any soft furniture (sofa/armchairs) and underneath the cushions
  • Hoover and mop the floor
  • Empty and re-bag bins

Bedrooms

  • Making the beds
  • Changing bed linen/sheets
  • Folding clothes and ironing
  • Wipe down all surfaces, polish mirrors, remove fingerprints
  • Clean door handles and around them, wipe light switches, and skirting boards
  • Hoover and mop the floor
